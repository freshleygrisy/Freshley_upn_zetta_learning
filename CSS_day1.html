Doctor Strange



13 October 2016

Ability / Super Power
A Master of the Mystic Arts, Doctor Strange has phenomenally powerful magical abilities that enable him to skillfully conjure myriad spells. Strange has been able to use his spells to bind opponents and create complex shields and barriers, among many other uses for both defense and attack. Strange discovers that certain artifacts can bolster magical spells and extend their benefits. The doctor uses of Sling Rings to create portals to cross great distances and to enter other realms.
